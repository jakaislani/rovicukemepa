State Of The Union: Esco Jouley Wikipedia Details

Esco Jouley is not mentioned on any of Wikipedia’s many pages. She was cast as one of the main characters in the second installment of the short series State of the Union.

Esco Jouléy is a New York City-based singer, actor, clown, dancer, movement artist, and creator. Esco was a resident actor at the historic Barter Theater for three and a half years.

She graduated from Penn State University with a BA in Integrative Arts and a minor in Dance. Moreover, she was a student at the American Musical and Dramatic Academy in New York City.

She was one of the few who received the Robert J. Prindle and Doris P. Prindle Memorial Award in creative art and the Lauren M. Becker Memorial Award.
