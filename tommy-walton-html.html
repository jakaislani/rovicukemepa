Tommy Walton - Age, Family, Bio

Chef known for being on Fox's Master Chef in 2015, where he finished in seventh place. His personal brand and style is luxury home cooking and champagne. He is also a fashion designer.

Before Fame

He taught an accessory design class at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. He also competed on "The Cut" which was a reality competition to design for Tommy Hilfiger in 2005.

Trivia

He created his Tom Walton's Caviar Club Instagram in 2014. His account combines his loves of fashion and food.

Family Life

He learned to cook from his grandmother who knew all of the Creole recipes. He also learned a lot from his aunt in Chicago.

Associated With

He was on Master Chef when Claudia Sandoval won the season.
