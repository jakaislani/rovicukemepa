Diego Klattenhoff height: How tall is Donald Ressler blacklist?

Canadian actor Diego Klattenhoff is well recognized for his roles as Mike Faber in the Showtime series Homeland and FBI Special Agent Donald Ressler in The Blacklist.

In addition to these notable parts, he has also played minor characters Shane Oman in Mean Girls, Derek in Whistler, and Ivan in Men in Trees.

Famous acting instructors David Rotenberg, Bruce Clayton, and Rae Ellen Bodie taught Klattenhoff acting in Canada. In Mean Girls in 2004, he made his acting debut.

Later, he had cameos in popular TV shows like Smallville and Stargate SG-1 as well as films like 2006’s Lucky Number Slevin.

He has been in recurring roles on several television programs since 2006, including NBC’s Mercy, Men in Trees on ABC, Whistler on CTV, At the Hotel on CBC Television, and Derek in Whistler.

In Homeland, Klattenhoff plays Mike Faber, a Marine Captain whose close friend reappears after going missing in Iraq eight years ago.

For their work on the program, Klattenhoff and the rest of the cast received nominations for two PAAFTJ Television Awards for Best Cast in a Drama Series and a Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Cast. He has also appeared in brief cameos on popular TV programs like Psych, Supernatural, 24, and Falling Skies.
