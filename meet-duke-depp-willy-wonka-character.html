Meet Duke Depp: The Willy Wonka Character on Tik Tok

If you’re a chocolate craving, Willy Wonka lover, Charlie and The Chocolate Factory fan, or just in for a trip down memory lane then meet 19-year-old, Duke Depp. Duke was born and raised in Utah’s capital, Salt Lake City, where he started out as a wedding photographer and quickly gained a following through social media. His photography skills gave him the opportunity to graduate two years early and travel around the world to take photos; including New York, Hawaii, and Greece. 

Duke’s creative platform and willingness to go for new, adventurous ideas quickly led to his famous TikTok account where he portrays the character, Willy Wonka. The 19 year old TikTok star chose Willy Wonka because of his already relatable and similar features to the chocolatier himself. Although most of us know the famous character as the strict factory owner and tour guide, Duke takes his videos to a whole new level bringing Willy Wonka to Generation Z to keep up with the latest dancing trends. While playing this character, he is sure to tie in the classics memories of the character like his famous chewing up, tour guide guests, and the adorable Umpa Lumpa’s. 

Willy Wonka’s followers thrived off of the account from the very beginning. Tiktok contains a wide variety of users, who have provided a mixture of trending content. But what is better than a classic chocolatier coming back? A classic chocolatier who interacts with all his fans and dances while doing it! Duke is able to interact with his fan base periodically through their comments, “dueting” of his videos, and live streaming as Willy Wonka. The viewers have shown their obsession by helping Duke reach hundreds of millions of views. Some have even openly developed a crush on Dukes’ Willy Wonka, while others are seeking entertainment and a trip down memory lane. 

Not many people can pull off the famous Willy Wonka character, but Duke has got it on point. The unique trends and viewer interaction that he brings to the account is what helps him continue making his mark on TikTok, especially his pink wig! If you’re in a chocolate craving and haven’t seen the newest Willy Wonka TikTok account, head over to Willy Wonka’s TikTok for Duke Depp’s voice and costume impersonation.
