Brycen Goodine Biography Details Revealed- Meet His Parents

Bryce Goodine is an African American college basketball player for the Providence Friars men’s basketball team. 

The Providence Friars men’s basketball team represents Providence College in NCAA Division I competition.

Bryce Goodine is one of their key players who play as a guard.

Brycen Goodine Age: How Old Is The Basketballer?

Providence’s breakout star, Brycen Goodine, is 22 years old.

He was born in September 1999 in New Bedford, Massachusetts, the United States.

In his Friar debut against Fairfield, Brycen had four points and three steals.

He scored eight points in the BIG EAST Tournament and grabbed five rebounds against DePaul.

Brycen Goodine Biography Explored

Brycen Goodine attended St. Andrews School in Barrington, R.I., coached by Mike Hart.

In 2018, Goodine was named the Gatorade Player of the Year for Rhode Island after leading St. Andrew’s to the NEPSAC Class AA championship game.

Moreover, he was also a member of the 2016 MIAA Division III State Championship team at Bishop Stang. He helped the team post a 19-3 record.

He was named the tournament MVP as St. Andrew’s team posted a 25-8 mark and won the NEPSAC Class AA title. In 2019, he earned the NEPSAC Class AA Player of the Year.

Brycen averaged 6.5 rounds per game, 21.5 points per game, and 5.5 assists per game in that year.

By ESPN.com, he was ranked No. 77 overall and the No. 13 shooting guard in 2019 and No. 92 overall and No. 21 shooting guard by Rivals.com.

Who Are Brycen Goodine Parents?

Brycen Goodine has two fathers: one biological father and a stepfather.

His biological father, Jonathan DePina, was a standout high school basketball player at South Boston and was expected to be part of a recruitment class that would have pushed Boston College basketball to new heights.

He ended up at UMass, where he played alongside Monty Mack and Shannon Crooks, two more Massachusetts superstars.

When Brycen was still a child, Jonathan and his ex-wife, Tiffan, divorced.

Tiffany Goodine Rodrigues married Brycen’s stepfather, Matt Rodrigues, on August 23, 2003.

Tiffany was the New Bedford, Massachusetts, region’s high school player of the year. She is currently the coach of a girls’ squad in Michael Carter-Williams’ AAU stable.

She is the mother of five children, who are basketball players.

Brycen is the oldest of four siblings, having three brothers and one sister. Caleb, Elijah, Jayden, and Olivia are their names.
