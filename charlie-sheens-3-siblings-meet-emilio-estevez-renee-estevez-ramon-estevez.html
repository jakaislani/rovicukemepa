Charlie Sheens 3 siblings: Meet Emilio Estevez, Rene Estevez, Ramon Estevez

Charlie Sheen is the son of actor Martin Sheen and artist Janet Templeton. He is from a family of six – his parents and four children including himself.

He happens to be the youngest son of his parents but the third born. He has two older siblings and a younger one. His siblings are Emilio Estevez, Renée Estevez and Ramon Estevez. Continue reading to know more about them.

Charlie Sheen’s 3 siblings: Meet Emilio Estevez, Renée Estevez, Ramon Estevez

Emilio Estevez happens to be the oldest among the four siblings. He was born to their parents on May 12, 1962, in Staten Island, New York, United States, hence, he is 61 years old. American actor and filmmaker.

He is a divorcee who has been married once with kids. He was married to Paula Abdul. They were married from 1992 to 1994. He has two children from his relationship with Carey Salley, a Wilhelmina model. His children are Taylor Levi Estévez and Paloma Estevez.

Ramon Estevez is the second child and son of their parents. He was born on August 7, 1963, in New York, New York, United States, hence, he is 60 years old now. He is an American actor and director who runs Estevez Sheen Productions.

He is the father of three children – Katherine Estévez, Luis Jr. Estévez, and Christopher Estévez. He is currently in a relationship with David Woodbury.

Renée Estevez is the youngest and only female among her siblings. She was born on April 2, 1967, in New York, New York, United States, hence, she is 56. She is an American actress and screenwriter.

She was married to Jason Thomas Federico. They tied the knot in 1997 and parted ways in divorce in 2011 in Los Angeles.
