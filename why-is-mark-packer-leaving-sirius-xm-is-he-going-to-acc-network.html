Why Is Mark Packer Leaving Sirius XM?

Mark Packer is leaving his work at Sirius XM and moving to a new platform of ACC Network.

On Wednesday, April 27, 2022, Mark Packer announced through his official Twitter handle that he will be leaving the Sirius platform and looking forward to starting a new project on a very different platform.

Thank you to all the fans and listeners of my SiriusXM show for the past 11+ years. While this chapter comes to a close, I’m extremely excited about my next venture. I will always appreciate your loyalty. In the meantime, I look forward to talking w/ you daily on @accnetwork.

— Mark Packer (@MarkPacker) April 28, 2022

Through his Twitter post, he thanked his listeners and followers for being a part of his 11 years long journey with Sirius XM.

He also announced that he had conducted the very last episode of his final show on ESPNU Radio on Sirius XM.

He further thanked his followers and audience for their loyalty and shared that he will be looking forward to hearing and talking with them again through his ACC Network work.

Mark started off with his show “Primetime with Packman” on WFNZ-FM and he worked there from 1997 till 2010 when the show was moved to Sirius XM.

He hosted ‘OffCampus’ in afternoons at ESPNU Radio while working on his show ‘Packer and Durham’ in the morning at ACC Network.
