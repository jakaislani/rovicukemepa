Bio, Early Life and Siblings

Troian Bellisario was born on October 28, 1985, in Los Angeles, California, the United States as Troian Avery Bellisario. She is the eldest daughter of television producer and screenwriter, Donald P. Bellisario, 85, and his actress wife, Deborah Pratt, 69.

Her parents were married from 1984 to 1991. Troian has one younger brother, Nicholas Bellisario, born on August 26, 1991. Also, she has five half-siblings named David Bellisario, Joy Bellisario-Jenkins, Michael Bellisario, Michael Bellisario, and Julie B. Watson from her dad's multiple relationships.

Bellisario studied at the Campbell Hall School in Studio City, California and also attended the Vassar College for a couple of months. However, she took a break due to her mental health. Later she enrolled at the University of Southern California, where she graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 2008.
