Official Mary Kate and John Luke Robertson wedding photos

Tonight is a very special season finale episode of A&E’s Duck Dynasty as Korie and Willie Robertson’s oldest son, John Luke Robertson, marries his high school sweetheart, Mary Kate McEacharn, in a beautiful ceremony held at the Robertson family farm in West Monroe, Louisiana. We previously shared candid photos of the ceremony shared by those in attendance, but now we have some of the official, professionally shot wedding pictures — and they are amazing!
