Tochukwu Oluehi (born 2 May 1987) is a Nigerian football goalkeeper for Medkila IL and the Nigeria women’s national football team.

Club career

Oluehi played for Bobruichanka Bobruisk in the 2013–14 UEFA Women’s Champions League. In April 2016, Oluehi and compatriot Cecilia Nku left Rivers Angels to join Norwegian Toppserien club Medkila IL.
